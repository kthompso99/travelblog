![The most common reason to come to this part of Zimbabwe is to see Victoria Falls. It's not the world's tallest (that's Angel in Venezuela), or the widest (varying candidates), or the highest flow rates (that's on the Congo River further north).

It's so huge (over a mile wide) that there's no (non-aerial) vantage point to see all or even much of it. This is maybe 5% of the falls. But regardless ... Gorgeous.](images/victoria-falls-23.jpg)

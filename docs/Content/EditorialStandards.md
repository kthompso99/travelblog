# A Practical Editing Workflow

When revising a post:

1. Highlight all sentences over 25 words.
2. Highlight all sentences starting with:
   - There
   - It
   - We were
3. Circle all vague adjectives.
4. Look for 3+ sentences in a row of similar length.
5. Cut 10% of total words.

That alone improves 90% of drafts.
